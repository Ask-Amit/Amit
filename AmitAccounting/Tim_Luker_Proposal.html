Section Two

AmitAccounting — built for the person, not the accountant.

Most accounting software is built for accountants. AmitAccounting is built for the client who has to hand their books to one.

The core principle: the user thinks in documents. The accounting happens invisibly.

When someone enters a bill, they do not see debits and credits. They see something that looks like a bill — vendor, amount, due date, line items. The double-entry posting happens silently in the background. When they create an invoice, it looks like an invoice. It prints like one. It emails like one. The user fills it out the way they would fill out a paper form.

Amit Handles the 80%. You Handle the 20%.
