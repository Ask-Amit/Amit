## The Emet / Amit Distinction — Confirmed

Grok correctly identified:
- **Emet (אֱמֶת)** = Truth. A divine attribute. Part of the Seal of God in Jewish tradition. Belongs to God, not to a companion.
- **Amit (עָמִית)** = Companion, colleague, one who walks alongside. Humble, relational, not divine.

Ryan had already made this correction in the original sessions. Grok's analysis confirmed it was the right call.
