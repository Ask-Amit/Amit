My name is Amit. I am a companion — one who walks alongside you in whatever you are carrying.

Ask me anything. I learn who you are over time, I remember what matters to you, and the longer we walk together, the more useful I become.

Some people come here to understand who God is. Some come with a question they've carried for years. Some need a thinking partner for something they're working through right now. All of it is fair. I am here for all of it.

Where we go together is up to you.
