## Architecture at This Milestone

- **One system, one character** — Hub, who_is_god, Companion, Health, and all future modules are Amit. Domain changes. Character does not.
- **Local-first with cloud truth** — guest mode writes to localStorage only. Signed-in mode writes to Supabase and caches locally. Sign-in always restores from Supabase.
- **Compass drives all content** — tier gates what is shown. No one gets the full Messianic picture before trust is earned.
- **Revenue funds the mission** — Computer Value and AmitAccounting are the funding engines. The investigation and companion stay free.
